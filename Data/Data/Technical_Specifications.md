# EV Architecture Comparison

## Overview

This project compares three real-world electric vehicles to understand differences in architecture, battery systems, motors, charging technologies, power electronics, and vehicle design.

## Vehicles

- Tata Nexon EV
- Hyundai Ioniq 5
- Ola S1 Pro
